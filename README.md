Project: Apple Sales Dashboard (Power BI)

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze apple sales data, focusing on sales volume and revenue across different apple varieties and time periods.

Dashboard features:

Dynamic filtering using Apple Variety slicer
Visualization of Sales Volume for selected variety
Display of key KPI: Total Revenue
Time-based analysis (Apple Sales Volume by Month)
Combined analysis of volume and revenue (Sales Volume and Revenue by Month)
All visuals update dynamically based on selected variety

Project goal:

Identify top-performing apple varieties
Analyze sales seasonality
Compare sales volume and revenue trends

Technologies:
Power BI, DAX, Power Query
